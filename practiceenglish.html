School Information

School Name:

Institución Educativa Manuel Uribe Ángel

School's Background:

Special school cycles night modality, "Ciclos Lectivos Integrados Especiales". This school also works in the mornings for Elementary, Middle and High School programs. CLEI is designed to be a flexible model for education of adults or youth that for any reason could not make it to the regular schedule.

CLEI Types:

Elementary (Básica primaria): Grades 1, 2 & 3 (CLEI 1), grades 4 & 5 (CLEI 2). Middle School (Básica secundaria): Grades 6 & 7 (CLEI 3), grades 8 & 9 (CLEI 4). High School (Media): Grade 10 (CLEI 5). and grade 11 (CLEI 6).

Groups Description:

CLEI program is compounded by students coming from great variety of backgrounds, most of these students have suffered of domestic financial difficulties having to work during the day to support household expenses, some of them have experienced sustance disorder, criminal background, indiscipline and adaptation problems. Nonetheless, these students show willingnes to overcome those difficult situations, getting support from the townhall and the municipal secretary of education for their wellness.

Project's Information

Project's Name:

innovative booklet for commonly used regular and irregular verbs.

Project's Description:

Students from CLEI-6 (Grade 11) that are about to get graduated will leave material for the ones that are coming next or they can leave it for themselves. The project consist in preparing an innovative list of commonly used regular and irregular verbs, that can work as a support for understanding better the list of verbs and identifying the ones that are frequently used in their past tense either regular or irregular.

Date:

August 15th/2023

Grade:

CLEI - 4-1 & 3-1

Subject:

English

Schedule:

18:30 - 21:30

Topic:

Review Don't and Doesn't and Household furniture objects

Goal Objective

Understand the use of "Don't and Doesn't" when singular and plural, likewise when it is related to third person. Understand furniture household objects and their locations.

Class Description:

Match review of "Don't" and "Doesn't" on the board, then a "Fill in the gaps" with the right form of Don't and Doesn't, finally translate the sentences. - Review the furniture household objects using the house locations to identify where the object is located at using "There is" and "There are". Then, with a "word search puzzle activity" they will reinforce the objects knowledge. Finally a pictionary game with those words in which students will have to figure out what is the furniture object drawn. For the next session, the guys can create sentences with them using "There is" "There Are" E.g. "There are" dishes in the kitchen/ "There is" a pillow on the Bed.

Pedagogical Reflection:

4-1 CLEIStudents were responsive to the class, however, they have huge lacks of vocabulary being so difficult for them to understand the session. Notwithstanding, they focused on the activity performing accurately and asking when needed. I noticed that some of the students get distracted so easy.3-1 CLEIstudents Students behave really good, they took part of the activities proposed, the class was clean, no novelties at all.
